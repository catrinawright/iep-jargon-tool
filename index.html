📖 Before You Begin — Background Knowledge Primer
What is the CFR?
The Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) is the official collection of rules published by federal agencies. Title 34 covers education. When you see 34 CFR § 300.43, it means: Title 34 (Education) → Part 300 (IDEA regulations) → Section 43 (Transition Services definition).
What does IDEA require of administrators?
As the designated LEA representative, you are legally responsible for committing school resources to the IEP. For transition-age students, this means ensuring measurable postsecondary goals based on assessment, with services to help the student reach those goals — including goals that may be affected by a criminal record.
What does WIOA add?
The Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA, 2014) requires state vocational rehabilitation agencies to provide Pre-Employment Transition Services (Pre-ETS) to students with disabilities, including instruction in self-advocacy — which includes knowledge of legal rights such as the right to pursue record expungement or sealing.
